9/34 Station Street Naremburn

Top floor north east facing retreat with leafy views

Ensconced in a beautiful leafy outlook, this north east facing apartment provides a light filled retreat on the top floor of a boutique complex. It's within the catchment for Cammeray Public School, close to St Leonards Station and a stroll to city buses.
- Revitalised interiors showcase contemporary finishes/fittings
- Open plan living zones maximise privacy, light and space
- Large wraparound terrace basks in private verdant outlook
- Granite finished kitchen equipped with stainless gas cooktop
- Well proportioned bedrooms, two appointed with built-in robes
- Master bedroom complete with a contemporary ensuite bathroom
- Chic full bathroom enhanced with separate bath and shower
- Security complex with intercom, internal laundry facilities
- Pet-friendly, double lock-up garage plus lock-up storage
- Close to parks, reserves and Royal North Shore Hospital

Strata levies $2,131pq
